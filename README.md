# 🌌 OneShot

> **Continuity-first AI storytelling.** Turn one idea into a living story universe.

OneShot is an AI-powered storytelling platform that transforms a single concept into a complete, interconnected universe. Generate characters, world-building, timelines, chapters, and lore while an intelligent continuity engine keeps every detail consistent across your story.

Whether you're writing a novel, web series, RPG campaign, comic, or cinematic universe, OneShot helps you create stories without breaking canon.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧬 **Story DNA** – Build and manage the core canon of your universe.
- 👥 **Character Intelligence** – Track personalities, motivations, relationships, and character arcs.
- 🌍 **World Building** – Organize locations, factions, cultures, magic systems, and lore.
- 📅 **Cinematic Timelines** – Create branching timelines and alternate realities.
- ✍️ **Chapter Workspace** – Write with AI assistance in a distraction-free editor.
- 🔍 **Continuity Engine** – Detect contradictions before they become plot holes.
- 📖 **Canon Management** – Review evidence, resolve conflicts, and maintain consistency.
- 🕒 **Version History** – Compare revisions, restore previous states, and evolve your universe safely.

---

## 🚀 Why OneShot?

Traditional AI writing tools generate text.

**OneShot builds universes.**

Every generated chapter understands your existing characters, lore, timeline, and world rules, ensuring your story remains consistent from the first page to the final chapter.
